Caketussy®

During the infamous worldwide pandemic, an idea made its way into the mind of Alberto, one of my closest friends. This urge to express himself found its first outlet in a name and a logo: Caketussy®. Initially conceived as a community to bring together other people who shared his artistic drive, pivotal ideal characterizing its first months of life, Caketussy® gradually evolved and understood its direction. In fact, only a few months after its creation, the first item was designed and made available to friends and fans: the caps, or cakecaps.


Alberto had many ideas but only two hands and a brain to bring them to life. To make matters more complicated, like me, he was also studying at university. In this situation, Silvio and I came into play. We threw ourselves fully into the project, trying to express our creativity through clothing, photography, videos, drawings and poetry.


Although over the years we completed various artistic and clothing projects, we never truly managed to define a clear mission until last year, when, with the help of Veronica, we decided to steer the Caketussy® project to sailing, a sport practiced by Alberto but to which Silvio and I had grown closer over the years, having lived in a place like Trieste (Italy) where sailing is an integral part of the culture.


Since then Caketussy® has become more of a sportswear brand that nonetheless retains its artistic identity striving to transform everything it touches into something unique, refined and appealing, both aesthetically and in the lifestyle it promotes.
