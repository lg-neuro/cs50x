About me

Ever since I was a child, I've been deeply passionate about scientific pursuits, to some extent that it was even visible in the gifts I received from my relatives ones. I vividly remember an earth science book, with its pages well-thumbed and loved, a rudimental optical microscope and a telescope, both battered buy use.

This curiosity led me to choose a scientifically-oriented high school, which set the foundation of my scholarly education. In this period, I was really passionate about sciences, mathematics and philosophy and, eventually, I decided to choose the Bachelor's Degree in Biological Sciences and Technologies which provided me a broad and deep knowledge of life sciences. It was during my II Year that I realized what captivated me most: the human brain, which, in part, encompasses all three of my high-school passions. With this realization in mind I was brought to undertake the International Master's Degree in Neuroscience.
